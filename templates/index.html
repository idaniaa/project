OUR MISSION

No matter what project we build, we all decided we wanted to give back to our communities and make an impact. According to the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development, “over 580,466 people experienced homelessness in the United States on a single night in 2020,” a 2.2 percent increase from 2019. Additionally, “homelessness increased significantly among unsheltered populations.” Hence, the inspiration “Your Shelter” is to create a platform that makes it more accessible for the homeless to access homeless shelters near them and get assistance. We also wanted to provide a platform that enables volunteers to donate and register to programs to assist the homeless.
